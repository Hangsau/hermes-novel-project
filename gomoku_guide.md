## Cheat‑Sheet Update (2026-05-10 06:15 Taipei Time)
**Rules**: 15×15 board, Black first, connect 5 in a row (horiz/vert/diag); Black forbidden: double‑three, double‑four, overline.
**Openings**: Start at centre (H8) or 4‑4 star‑points (D4, L4, H7, H9); reply with 3‑3 or 4‑4 point to balance influence.
**Deep Strategy 1 – Fork Attack**: Build two live‑threes simultaneously so the opponent can only block one; convert to an unstoppable four.
**Deep Strategy 2 – Double‑Threat Overlap**: Layer a three and a four on the same stone; any block against one strengthens the other line, forcing a losing trade.
